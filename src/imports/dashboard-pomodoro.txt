Left Sidebar: Minimalist navigation menu. Menu items: Home, My Roadmap, Achievements, AI Mock Interview. At the bottom of the sidebar, add an eye-catching Upgrade Banner showing two tiers: "Basic: 89k VND/mo" and "Premium: 199k VND/course".

Main Content (Left/Center): A clean, card-based "Today's Tasks" list. No clutter.

Main Content (Right): A prominent, large Pomodoro timer widget. Show a glowing, animated countdown ring displaying "25:00". Below the timer, a bold "Start Focus" button.

2. Prompt: Giao diện AI Roadmap (Gamification như Duolingo)
Biến lộ trình học khô khan thành một bản đồ game cuốn hút để chốt sale gói 89k.

Context: Design a gamified AI learning roadmap interface for a student app.
Style: Playful, vibrant, Light Mode. Highly inspired by Duolingo's path UI and Coursera's milestones. Use soft rounded corners and bright primary colors (leaf green, sunny yellow, sky blue).

Layout Requirements:

Top Bar: Display a glowing "Fire Streak" counter (e.g., 12 days) and a "Coins/Gems" counter.

The Roadmap: A vertical, winding path curving down the screen.

Milestone Nodes: Place large, colorful circular buttons along the path.

Passed nodes: Bright yellow with a glowing star icon.

Failed/Retry nodes: Highlighted with a subtle red alert outline.

Current node: Enlarged, pulsing, with a floating "Start Learning" tooltip.

3. Prompt: Giao diện AI Mock Interview (Chân thực như ChatGPT Voice)
Tạo cảm giác "áp lực phòng thi" chân thực cho tính năng vũ khí hạng nặng của gói 199k.

Context: Design an AI Mock Interview screen for a career preparation app.
Style: Immersive, modern, Light Mode. Inspired by the voice interface of ChatGPT Voice and Pi AI. Do not use a standard text chat format.

Layout Requirements:

Center Stage: A large, dynamic, animated voice wave or a glowing orb in the center of the screen, indicating the "AI HR Manager" is currently speaking and analyzing.

Bottom Action Area: A massive, prominent microphone button with the text "Tap to Speak" or "Hold to Answer".

Top Header: Simple text showing "Mock Interview: Frontend Developer" and a subtle recording timer (e.g., "04:12"). Keep the rest of the screen entirely empty and distraction-free to build a tense, realistic interview atmosphere.

4. Prompt: Giao diện AI CV Builder (Chuyên nghiệp như Novoresume)
Tính năng xịn nhất, yêu cầu chia đôi màn hình để thấy ngay kết quả biến đổi.

Context: Design an AI-powered CV Builder web interface for students.
Style: Professional, highly productive workspace, Light Mode. Inspired by Novoresume and Enhancv.

Layout Requirements:

Split-Screen Layout (50/50):

Left Panel (AI Assistant): A clean chat interface where the AI prompts the user: "I see you just completed the React Roadmap. Let's add that to your CV!". Include input fields for the user to confirm their newly acquired skills.

Right Panel (Live Preview): A real-time, high-fidelity preview of an ATS-friendly CV document (A4 paper aspect ratio). The UI should visually indicate that the CV is automatically updating and generating professional bullet points as the user chats on the left.

Top Right: A premium "Export to PDF" button.
